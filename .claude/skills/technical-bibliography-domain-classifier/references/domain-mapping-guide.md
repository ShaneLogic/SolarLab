## Multi-Domain Handling

When a reference matches multiple domains:

1. **Prioritize by journal scope** - Specialized journals take precedence
2. **Consider primary topic** - Main research focus determines domain
3. **Note cross-domain nature** - Document secondary domains

### Common Overlaps

- Optical Properties ↔ Semiconductor Physics (exciton studies)
- Surface Science ↔ Electronic Devices (interface studies)
- Materials Science ↔ Photovoltaics (thin film materials)
- Semiconductor Physics ↔ Electronic Devices (device physics)
