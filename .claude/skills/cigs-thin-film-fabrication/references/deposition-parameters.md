# Detailed Deposition Parameters

## Co-evaporation Process Control

### Flux Rates (typical)
- Cu: 0.5-2.0 nm/s
- In: 0.5-2.0 nm/s
- Ga: 0.2-1.0 nm/s
- Se: 5-20 nm/s (excess)

### Three-Stage Process
1. **Stage 1:** (In,Ga)2Se3 deposition at substrate temperature
2. **Stage 2:** Cu deposition until Cu-rich
3. **Stage 3:** (In,Ga)2Se3 + Se deposition to final composition

## Selenization Process

### Precursor Options
- **Sputtered metals:** Cu/In/Ga layers
- **Electroplated:** Cu/In/Ga from aqueous solutions
- **Nanoparticle inks:** Cu-In-Ga particles in solvent

### Reaction Profile
- Ramp to 400-500°C at 5-10°C/min
- Hold at temperature for 30-60 min
- Cool down under Se atmosphere

## TCO Deposition

### ZnO:Al Sputtering Parameters
- **Power:** 100-500 W (rf)
- **Pressure:** 0.5-5 mTorr
- **Ar:O2 ratio:** 99:1 to 95:5
- **Substrate temperature:** Room temperature to 200°C

### ITO Sputtering Parameters
- **Power:** 50-200 W (rf or dc)
- **Pressure:** 1-10 mTorr
- **Ar:O2 ratio:** 98:2 to 90:10
- **Substrate temperature:** 200-400°C
