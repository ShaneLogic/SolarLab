# Optical Design Variables Reference

## QE - Quantum Efficiency
- Definition: Ratio of collected electrons to incident photons
- Range: 0 to 1 (or 0-100%)
- Wavelength dependent

## j(λ) - Photocurrent Density
- Units: mA/cm² (spectrally resolved)
- Measured under monochromatic illumination
- Integral over spectrum gives total Jsc

## f(λ) - Incident Photon Flux
- Units: photons/(s·cm²·nm)
- Standard solar spectrum: AM1.5G
- Varies with wavelength

## A(λ) - Absorptance
- Definition: Fraction of incident light absorbed
- Range: 0 to 1
- For single pass: A = 1 - exp(-αd)

## α(λ) - Absorption Coefficient
- Units: cm⁻¹
- Material and wavelength dependent
- Typical a-Si:H: 10³-10⁵ cm⁻¹ across visible

## d - Layer Thickness
- Units: nm or μm
- Typical a-Si:H i-layer: 200-500 nm
- Affects both electrical and optical properties

## Light Trapping Factor
- Enhancement factor due to multiple passes
- Ideal limit: 4n² (Yablonovitch limit)
- Practical values: 5-20× depending on texturing
