# Optical Properties and Photoelectric Effects

## When to Use
- Studying photon interactions with semiconductors
- Analyzing photoconductivity or photovoltaic devices
- Measuring or calculating optical parameters (refractive index, absorption coefficient)
- Investigating luminescence and emission phenomena
- Performing optical spectroscopy or modulation spectroscopy

## Core Analysis Areas

### 1. Fundamental Optical Parameters
Measure or calculate key optical properties:
- Index of refraction
- Optical absorption and absorption coefficient
- Optical absorption spectrum
- Optical dielectric constant
- Reflectance and transmission properties

### 2. Photoelectric Phenomena
Analyze carrier generation and transport due to light:
- **Photoconductivity**: Light-induced increase in conductivity
  - Intrinsic photoconductivity
  - Persistent photoconductivity
- **Photodetectivity/Photosensitivity**: Device response to light
- **Photo-emf and Photocurrent**: Generated voltage/current from light
- **Photovoltaic effect**: Direct conversion of light to electricity in devices

### 3. Luminescence and Emission
Study light emission from materials:
- **Luminescence**: General emission phenomena
  - Laser-excited luminescence
- **Phosphorescence**: Delayed emission
- **Phosphor materials**: Emissive materials for displays/lighting

### 4. Advanced Optical Analysis Techniques
Apply sophisticated measurement and analysis methods:
- Measurement of optical parameters
- Interference effects and patterns
- Modulation reflection spectroscopy
- Kramers-Kronig analysis for complex optical constants

## Key Relationships
- Absorption depends on band gap (Eg) - requires hν ≥ Eg
- Photoconductivity requires both carrier generation and transport
- Photovoltaic effect requires built-in electric field (e.g., p-n junction)
- Luminescence requires radiative recombination pathways

## Output
- Absorption coefficient (cm⁻¹)
- Photocurrent or photovoltage values
- Luminescence spectrum intensity vs wavelength
- Refractive index values
- Spectroscopic measurement data
