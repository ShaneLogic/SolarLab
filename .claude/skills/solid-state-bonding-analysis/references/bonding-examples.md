# Worked Examples for Bonding Calculations

## Example 1: NaCl Ionic Bond Potential Energy

Given:
- re = 2.8 Å
- m = 9
- q = elementary charge

Minimum potential energy: Vmin ≈ -5 eV

## Example 2: Carbon Bond Length Trend

| Bond Type | Length (Å) |
|-----------|------------|
| C-C (single) | 1.54 |
| C=C (double) | 1.38 |
| C≡C (triple) | 1.21 |

Bond length decreases with increasing bonding valency.

## Example 3: Born-Haber Cycle for NaCl

Energy contributions:
1. W_subl (Na sublimation): +1.1 eV
2. W_ion (Na ionization): +5.1 eV
3. W_diss/2 (Cl₂ dissociation): +1.2 eV
4. W_elaff (Cl electron affinity): -3.6 eV
5. W_solid (heat of formation): -4.3 eV

H₀ = -4.3 - [1.1 + 5.1 + 1.2 + (-3.6)] = -8.1 eV (per molecule)
