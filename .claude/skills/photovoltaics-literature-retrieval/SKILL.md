# Photovoltaics Literature Retrieval

Use this skill when searching for foundational and advanced references in solar energy and photovoltaics research.

## When to Use

- Writing literature reviews for solar energy or photovoltaics
- Finding key references for thin-film solar cells (CdTe, CIGS)
- Searching for solar cell characterization and measurement techniques
- Locating resources for photovoltaic device optimization
- Investigating advances in solar energy technology

## Key Research Domains

- **Thin Film Solar Cells**: CdTe, CIGS technologies
- **Solar Cell Characterization**: Measurement standards and techniques
- **Device Performance**: Optimization and simulation insights
- **General Solar Advances**: Historical and recent developments

## Retrieval Procedure

1. Identify the specific research domain or topic
2. Consult the domain-specific reference list in `references/photovoltaics-citations.md`
3. Select relevant citations based on:
   - Authors specializing in the target domain
   - Publication dates appropriate to research needs
   - Specific technologies or measurement techniques of interest
4. Verify publication details before using in bibliography
