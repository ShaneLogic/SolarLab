# Lead Chalcogenides (PbS, PbSe, PbTe) Band Parameters

## Key Structural Feature
Unlike most direct gap semiconductors where the valence band maximum and conduction band minimum are at the Γ-point, lead chalcogenides have both extrema at the L-point.
