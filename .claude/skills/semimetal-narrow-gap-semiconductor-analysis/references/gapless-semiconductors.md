# Gapless Semiconductors

## Definition
Materials where the top of valence band touches the bottom of conduction band, resulting in:
- Density of states at Fermi level is ZERO at T = 0K
- Contrast with semimetals: semimetals have very small but NON-ZERO density of states

## Special Case: Tailing Band States
- Tailing states = band states extending into bandgap due to disturbed neighborhood of lattice defects
- These states can overlap with conduction band, affecting electronic properties
