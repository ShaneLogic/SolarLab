# Gray Tin (α-Sn)

## Classification
Narrow gap semiconductor (diamond cubic structure)

## Bandgap
- Range: 0–0.08 eV
- Determined by magneto-optical experiments (Pidgeon 1969)

## Notes
- The very small bandgap places gray tin at the boundary between semiconductors and semimetals
- Temperature and pressure can induce semiconductor-to-metal transitions
