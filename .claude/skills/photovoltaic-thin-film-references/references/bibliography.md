## Research Application Notes

- For efficiency optimization studies: Focus on Palm et al. (2004), Paulson et al. (2003), and Pan et al. (2006)
- For deposition technique development: Consult Orbey et al. (1998) and Orgassa et al. (2003)
- For historical context and technology evolution: Reference Perlin (1999) and conference proceedings
- For fundamental semiconductor properties: Review Oparaku et al. (1990)
