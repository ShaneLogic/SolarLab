# Detailed Defect Energy Levels

## Shallow Defects (Photoluminescence)
- Acceptor 1: ~30 meV from valence band
- Acceptor 2: ~60 meV from valence band
- Acceptor 3: ~100 meV from valence band
- Donor: ~10 meV from conduction band

## Deep Defects (Capacitance)
- N2 defect: 250-300 meV from valence band
- 800 meV defect: 800 meV from valence band
  - Mid-gap position for Eg > 1.6 eV
  - Becomes recombination center at high Ga content

## Bandgap Calculation
Eg(x) = 1.04 + 0.67x - 0.14x(1-x) eV
where x = Ga/(In+Ga) fraction

## Critical Defects
- V_Se: Amphoteric, explains metastability
- III_Cu (In_Cu, Ga_Cu): DX center related, limits doping
- N2: Benign deep defect
- 800 meV: Problematic at high Ga content
