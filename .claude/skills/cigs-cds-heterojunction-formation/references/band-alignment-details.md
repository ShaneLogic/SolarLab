# Band Alignment Details

## Energy Band Parameters

### CdS Properties
- Type: n-type
- Band gap (Eg): 2.4 eV

### ZnO Properties
- Type: n++ (heavily doped)
- Band gap (Eg): 3.2 eV

## Band Offset Values

### Conduction Band Offset (ΔEc)
- Definition: Positive ΔEc means CdS CBM is higher than Cu(InGa)Se2 CBM
- Forms a "spike" in the conduction band
- Critical threshold: 0.5 eV

### Valence Band Offset (ΔEv)
- Value: -0.9 eV (consistent across all film types)
- Creates a barrier for hole transport (beneficial)

### ZnO/CdS Conduction Band Offset
- Value: -0.3 eV
- ZnO CBM is lower than CdS CBM

## Transport Mechanisms

### For ΔEc < 0.5 eV
- Electrons cross via thermionic emission
- Efficient collection expected

### For ΔEc > 0.5 eV
- Spike acts as barrier
- Collection impeded
- Jsc and FF sharply reduced

## Historical Device Evolution

1. **Original structure**: p-CuInSe2 / n-CdS
2. **Improved structure**: p-CuInSe2 / undoped CdS / doped CdS
3. **Modern structure**: p-Cu(InGa)Se2 / undoped CdS (~50nm) / doped ZnO
