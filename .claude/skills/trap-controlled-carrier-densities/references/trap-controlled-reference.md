# Trap-Controlled Carrier Densities - Reference

## Detailed Equations

### Reaction Kinetic Equations (Eqs 31.25-31.27)
The system is described by reaction kinetic equations for:
- Electron trapping and release
- Recombination processes
- Generation processes

### Neutrality Condition
The charge neutrality condition relates free carriers, trapped carriers, and recombination centers.

### Simplified Neutrality (Eq 31.29)
Under predominant trapping:
$$n_t \approx n$$

### Modified Carrier Density Relation (Eq 31.31)
The nonlinear relation between $n$ and $g_o$ includes a modified bottleneck relation in square brackets.

### Low Generation Regime Formula (Eq 31.32)
When $n_t \approx N_t \gg n$:
$$n = g_o c_{rv} \frac{N_r c_{cr}}{c_{rv} N_r N_t - g_o}$$

## Variable Definitions

| Symbol | Type | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| $n$ | density | Free electron density |
| $n_t$ | density | Density of trapped electrons |
| $N_t$ | density | Density of electron traps |
| $N_r$ | density | Density of recombination centers |
| $g_o$ | rate | Optical generation rate |
| $p$ | density | Hole density |
| $c_{rv}$ | coefficient | Recombination coefficient |
| $c_{cr}$ | coefficient | Capture coefficient |

## Model Constraints
- Assumes predominant carrier trapping
- Simple model with electron traps and recombination centers
- Valid for n-type photoconductors
