# Multi-Level Center Energy Examples

## Binding Energy Scaling Formula

For Coulomb-attractive centers with charge Z:
```
E_binding ∝ Z² × E_hydrogenic
```

Where E_hydrogenic is the binding energy of a single-charge donor/acceptor.

## Example Values

### Quasi-Hydrogen Estimates (Simple Model)
- **Germanium**: ~50 meV for 2nd donor level
- **Silicon**: ~120 meV for 2nd donor level

### Actual Measured Energies (Si:S - Sulfur in Silicon)
- **1st donor level**: 302 meV
- **2nd donor level**: 587 meV

Note: Actual values are significantly higher than simple estimates, indicating strong central cell effects.

## Implications

1. **Hydrogen-like model validity**: Breaks down for Z > 1 centers
2. **Central cell potential**: Must be included for accurate calculations
3. **Tight binding**: Eigenfunctions overlap with core states
4. **Experimental verification**: Essential due to theoretical limitations
