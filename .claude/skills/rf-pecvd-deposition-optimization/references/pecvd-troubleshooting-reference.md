# RF-PECVD Troubleshooting Reference

## Common Issues and Solutions

### Powder Formation
**Signs:**
- Visible powder in chamber
- Hazy appearance on films
- Rapid drop in FF

**Causes:**
- Power density > 100 mW/cm²
- Pressure too high
- Excessive SiH₄ concentration

**Solutions:**
- Reduce RF power
- Lower chamber pressure
- Increase H₂ dilution

### Contamination
**Detection:**
- SIMS analysis
- FTIR spectroscopy
- Electrical degradation

**Sources:**
- Air leaks (oxygen, nitrogen)
- Outgassing (carbon)
- Previous film residue

**Prevention:**
- Regular leak checks
- Chamber baking
- Proper cleaning procedures

### Uniformity Issues
**Measurement:**
- Thickness mapping
- Spectroscopic ellipsometry
- Uniformity specification: ±5%

**Causes:**
- Incorrect electrode spacing
- Gas flow distribution issues
- Temperature gradients

**Solutions:**
- Adjust spacing to 1-2 cm
- Optimize gas inlet design
- Improve temperature uniformity

## Process Monitoring

### In-situ Monitoring
- Optical emission spectroscopy
- Interferometry for thickness
- Plasma impedance monitoring

### Ex-situ Characterization
- FTIR (hydrogen bonding)
- UV-Vis (bandgap)
- Electrical testing (mobility, FF)

## Maintenance Schedule

- Daily: Visual inspection, leak check
- Weekly: Chamber cleaning, electrode check
- Monthly: Gas line purging, full system check
- Quarterly: Major maintenance, calibration
