# Orbital Configuration Reference

## Common Transition Metal Configurations

### 3d Series
- Sc: 3d¹ 4s²
- Ti: 3d² 4s²
- V: 3d³ 4s²
- Cr: 3d⁵ 4s¹ (exception)
- Mn: 3d⁵ 4s²
- Fe: 3d⁶ 4s²
- Co: 3d⁷ 4s²
- Ni: 3d⁸ 4s²
- Cu: 3d¹⁰ 4s¹ (exception)
- Zn: 3d¹⁰ 4s²

### 4d and 5d Series
Follow similar patterns with expanded orbitals.

### 4f Rare Earth
- La: 4f¹ 5d¹ 6s²
- Ce: 4f¹ 5d¹ 6s² to 4f² 6s²
- ... (varies across series)

### 5f Actinides
- Ac: 6d¹ 7s²
- Th: 6d² 7s²
- U: 5f³ 6d¹ 7s²
- ... (complex due to f-orbital participation)

## Symmetry Types

### Common Crystal Field Symmetries
- **Tetrahedral**: T_d symmetry, common in zinc blende structures
- **Octahedral**: O_h symmetry, common in perovskites
- **Orthorhombic**: Lower symmetry, distorted sites
- **Trigonal**: C_3v or D_3d symmetry

## Crystal Field Splitting

### Octahedral Field
- t_2g orbitals (lower energy)
- e_g orbitals (higher energy)
- Splitting parameter: Δ_o

### Tetrahedral Field
- e orbitals (lower energy)
- t_2 orbitals (higher energy)
- Splitting parameter: Δ_t ≈ 4/9 Δ_o
