# Detailed Growth Parameters

## Vapor Phase Growth
- **CdS sublimation:** Forms platelets through spontaneous nucleation
- **MBE typical rates:** 0.1-1.0 μm/hour
- **Reacting atmosphere examples:** H2S for II-VI compounds, As4 for III-V compounds

## Melt Growth
- **Czochralski pull rates:** 1-100 mm/hour depending on material
- **Bridgeman cooling rates:** 1-100°C/hour
- **Seed orientation:** Critical for single crystal quality

## Solution Growth
- **Temperature lowering rate:** 0.1-10°C/day for high quality
- **Solvent selection criteria:** Solubility, chemical compatibility, purity

## Chemical Reaction Growth
- **Spray deposition:** Substrate temperature 200-600°C
- **Gel aging:** Days to weeks for optimal diffusion
