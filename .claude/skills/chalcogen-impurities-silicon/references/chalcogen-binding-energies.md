## Pressure Coefficients

- S/Se ground state: ~ -1.7 to -1.8 x 10^-8 eV/Pa
- Larger than hydrogen-like centers, indicating connection to X-valley conduction bands

## Hydrogen-like Reference Values

- E_Hydrogen: ~11.75 meV
- 2p0 state: ~11.5 meV
