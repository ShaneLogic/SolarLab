# Optical Phonon Scattering Formulas and Data

## Mobility Formula (Eq. 17.18)

The carrier mobility from optical phonon scattering is given by:

μ = [expression involving ρ, D_o, Φ(T)]

Where:
- μ = carrier mobility (cm²/V·s)
- ρ = semiconductor density (kg/m³)
- D_o = optical deformation potential (eV)
- Φ(T) = temperature-dependent auxiliary function

## Optical Deformation Potential (Eq. 17.19)

The optical deformation potential D_o is defined as:

D_o = f(δr)

Where δr is the change in interatomic distance.

### Temperature Dependence
- At T = θ/10: Φ(T) ≈ 10⁴-10⁵
- At T ≈ θ: Φ(T) ≈ 1
- For T < 0.3θ: Nearly exponential decrease with increasing temperature
