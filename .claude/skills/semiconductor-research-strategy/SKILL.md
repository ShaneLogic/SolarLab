# Semiconductor Research Strategy

## When to Use
- Planning research programs for new semiconductor materials
- Designing semiconductors with specific properties for improved devices
- Establishing research priorities and methodology

## Core Strategy

Focus research efforts on three main areas:

1. **Synthesize New Semiconducting Materials**
   - Develop novel compounds with targeted properties
   - Explore new material systems beyond existing options

2. **Use Sophisticated Growth Methods**
   - Implement advanced crystal growth techniques
   - Optimize deposition processes for quality and reproducibility

3. **Apply Theoretical Analysis**
   - Use computational models to predict material properties
   - Identify potentially interesting target materials before synthesis
   - Guide experimental efforts with theoretical insights

## Research Workflow

1. **Theoretical Prediction**
   - Use computational models to identify promising materials
   - Predict electronic, optical, and structural properties

2. **Material Synthesis**
   - Develop synthesis routes for predicted materials
   - Optimize growth conditions for desired properties

3. **Characterization and Validation**
   - Measure actual material properties
   - Compare with theoretical predictions
   - Refine models based on experimental results
