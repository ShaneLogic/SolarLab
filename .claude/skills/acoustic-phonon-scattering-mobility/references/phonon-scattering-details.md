# Acoustic Phonon Scattering - Detailed Analysis

## Mobility Formula (Full Expression)
```
μ_n,ac = √(38π) × m_n^(5/2) / (e × k × h × T^(4/3) × c_l^(3/2) × Ξ²)
```

## Temperature Dependence
- At higher temperatures: μ ∝ T^(-3/2)
- This is characteristic of acoustic phonon scattering in direct bandgap semiconductors
- NOT observed in indirect bandgap semiconductors due to intervalley scattering

## Deformation Potential Considerations
- The deformation potential Ξ used in this formula has slowly varying components in space
- Alternative approach by Ginter and Mycielski (1970) includes potential varying with lattice periodicity
- Ginter and Mycielski's approach is more appropriate for shorter wavelength phonons

## Elastic Tensor Components

- Anisotropic nature leads to directional dependence
