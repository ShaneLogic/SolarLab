# Semiconductor Terms Index (S-Z)

## When to Use
Use this skill when:
- Searching for semiconductor physics concepts starting with S, T, U, V, W, X, Y, or Z
- Needing to locate page references for technical terminology
- Looking up specific phenomena, materials, or theoretical concepts

## How to Use

1. **Identify the search term**
   - Determine the first letter of your term
   - Confirm it falls within S-Z range

2. **Search the index**
   - Look for exact term matches

3. **Retrieve page numbers**
