# Flux Materials for Recrystallization

## Common Flux Materials

| Semiconductor | Flux Material | Effect |
|---------------|---------------|--------|
| CdS | Cuprous chloride (CuCl) | Cu incorporation, increased photoconductivity |
| CdSe | CdCl2 | Grain growth enhancement |
| ZnS | NaCl, KCl | Lower processing temperature |

## Flux Application Guidelines

1. Apply sparingly - excess flux causes surface defects
2. Surface application typically sufficient
3. Heating temperature must exceed flux melting point
4. Cooling rate affects final grain size

## Dopant Incorporation

When flux elements incorporate into crystal:
- Verify dopant is benign or desirable for application
- Measure resulting electrical properties
- Copper in CdS: increases photoconductivity (often desirable)
- Unwanted dopants may require additional purification
