# Recrystallization Mechanism Details

## Physical Parameters

*   **Activation Energy (Ea):** Approximately 2.5 eV.

## Mechanism Steps

*   **Bond Diffusion:** The process occurs primarily through the diffusion of dangling bonds within the amorphous network.
*   **Mediation:** Floating bonds act as mediators during the recrystallization process.
*   **Structural Outcome:** The final state converts all atomic rings into six-member rings, characteristic of a crystalline structure.
