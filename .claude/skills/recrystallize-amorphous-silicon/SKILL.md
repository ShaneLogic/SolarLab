# Recrystallize Amorphous Silicon

Execute this procedure to convert amorphous silicon layers into a crystalline structure.

## Procedure

1. **Verify System Configuration**
   - Confirm the presence of an amorphous silicon (a-Si) layer.
   - Ensure the layer is situated on a crystalline silicon (c-Si) substrate.

2. **Initiate Energy Input**
   - Apply thermal annealing or direct energy input to the system.
   - Ensure the energy provided exceeds the activation threshold required for bond diffusion.

3. **Execute Recrystallization**
   - Allow dangling bonds to diffuse through the amorphous layer.
   - Facilitate the mediation of floating bonds to drive the structural change.

4. **Validate Structural State**
   - Confirm the conversion of the atomic structure.
   - Verify that the resulting structure consists entirely of six-member rings, indicating successful crystallization.
