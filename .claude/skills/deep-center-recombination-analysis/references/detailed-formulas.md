# Detailed Formulas for Deep Center Recombination

## Equation References

### Detailed Balance for Unrelaxed Trap (Reference)
Used to obtain s_∞ in pre-exponential factor calculation.

### Henry and Lang (1977) Model
Foundation for multiphonon emission theory at deep centers.

### Sumi (1983) Model
Extension of configuration coordinate model for deep-level recombination.

## Variable Definitions

| Variable | Type | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| EB | Energy | Activation energy for trapping (EB1 for electrons, EB2 for holes) |
| σ_∞ | Area | Pre-exponential capture cross section |
| S | Integer | Huang-Rhys factor (number of phonons emitted) |
| v_c | Integer | Valley degeneracy in conduction band |
| v_t | Integer | Degeneracy of deep trap level |
| En | Energy | Optical excitation energy |
| EB | Energy | Crossover energy between upper and lower curves |
| hω | Energy | Phonon energy |
| A | Dimensionless | Electron-lattice coupling parameter |
| wr | Frequency | Defect eigenfrequency of breathing mode |
