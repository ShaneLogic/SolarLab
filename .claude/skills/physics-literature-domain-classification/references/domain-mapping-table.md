# Detailed Domain Classification Reference

## Complete Domain Taxonomy

### 1. Semiconductor Devices & Technology

**Keywords Pattern:**
- Primary: Device, Junction, Diode, Transistor
- Application: Solar, Cell, Photovoltaic, Detector
- Processing: Fabrication, Deposition, Etching

**Signature Publications:**
- S.M. Sze - *Physics of Semiconductor Devices* (Wiley)
- S.M. Sze - *Physics of Semiconductor Devices*, 2nd edn. (Wiley, New York, 1981)
- K. Takahashi - *Amorphous Silicon Solar Cells* (North-Holland, Amsterdam, 1986)

**Journal Indicators:**
- IEEE Trans. Electron Devices
- IEEE J. Quantum Electron.
- Solar Energy Materials

### 2. Surface Science & Interfaces

**Keywords Pattern:**
- Primary: Surface, Interface, Boundary
- Technique: Photoemission, EXAFS, LEED, STM, AES
- Phenomenon: Reconstruction, Adsorption, Segregation

**Signature Publications:**
- W.E. Spicer - Multiple papers on photoemission (Phys. Rev. series)
- J. Tersoff - Surface theory papers (Phys. Rev. B)
- E. Taglauer - *Semiconductor Interfaces: Formation and Properties*

**Journal Indicators:**
- Surface Science (Surf. Sci.)
- Journal of Vacuum Science & Technology
- Physical Review B (interface sections)

### 3. Fundamental Solid State Theory

**Keywords Pattern:**
- Primary: Theory, Band, Exciton, Polaron
- Concept: Defect, Impurity, Doping, Carrier
- Method: Ab initio, Tight-binding, k·p

**Signature Publications:**
- A.M. Stoneham - *Theory of Defects in Solids* (Oxford, 1975)
- S.T. Pantelides - *Deep Centers in Semiconductors* (Gordon and Breach, 1988)
- J.C. Phillips - *Bonds and Bands in Semiconductors* (Academic Press, 1973)
- R. Peierls - *Quantum Theory of Solids* (Oxford, 1955)

**Journal Indicators:**
- Physical Review (general)
- Philosophical Magazine
- Solid State Communications

### 4. Amorphous & Disordered Materials

**Keywords Pattern:**
- Primary: Amorphous, Non-crystalline, Glass, Disordered
- Structure: Network, Random, Tetrahedral
- Property: Staebler-Wronski, Switching, Memory

**Signature Publications:**
- R.A. Street - *Hydrogenated Amorphous Silicon* (Cambridge, 1991)
- S.T. Pantelides (ed.) - *Amorphous Silicon and Related Materials* (World Scientific, 1989)
- S.R. Ovshinsky - Multiple switching effect papers (Phys. Rev. Lett.)

**Key Author Publications:**
- S.R. Ovshinsky - Phys. Rev. Lett. **21**, 1450 (1968) - Ovshinsky effect
- W.E. Spear & P.G. Le Comber - Amorphous Si semiconductor papers
- J. Stuke - Amorphous silicon foundational work

**Journal Indicators:**
- Journal of Non-Crystalline Solids
- Phys. Rev. B (amorphous sections)
- Applied Physics Letters (a-Si:H)

### 5. Optical Properties & Spectroscopy

**Keywords Pattern:**
- Primary: Optical, Dielectric, Refractive, Absorption
- Technique: Spectroscopy, Ellipsometry, Raman, PL, CL
- Concept: Exciton, Polariton, Plasmon

**Signature Publications:**
- E.D. Palik - *Handbook of Optical Constants of Solids* (Academic Press, 1985)
- S.I. Pekar - *Untersuchungen über die Elektronentheorie der Kristalle* (1954)

**Key Author Publications:**
- H.R. Philipp - Optical constants of semiconductors (Phys. Rev.)
- A. Otto - Surface polarons (Z. Phys.)
- L. Ozawa - *Cathodoluminescence* (1990)

### 6. Crystallography & Defects

**Keywords Pattern:**
- Primary: Crystal, Lattice, Structure, Defect
- Method: Growth, Epitaxy, Diffraction, TEM
- Type: Dislocation, Stacking fault, Grain boundary

**Signature Publications:**
- B. Pamplin - *Crystal Growth*, 2nd edn. (Pergamon, 1980)
- W.G. Pfann - *Zone Melting* (Wiley, 1958)
- E. Parthe - *Crystal Chemistry of Tetrahedral Structures* (1964)
- P. Villars, L.D. Calvert - *Pearson's Handbook of Crystallographic Data* (ASM, 1985)

**Key Author Publications:**
- S.T. Pantelides - Rev. Mod. Phys. **50**, 797 (1978) - Defects review
- Yu.A. Osip'yan - Dislocation effects (Sov. Phys.-JETP Lett.)

### 7. Characterization Techniques

**Keywords Pattern:**
- Microscopy: TEM, SEM, STM, AFM
- Spectroscopy: EXAFS, XPS, AES, SIMS
- Diffraction: XRD, LEED, RHEED

**Signature Publications:**
- J. Stöhr - EXAFS methodology papers
- J. Stroscio, E. Eigler - *Scanning Tunneling Microscopy* (1993)
- J.B. Pendry - *Low Energy Electron Diffraction* (1972)
