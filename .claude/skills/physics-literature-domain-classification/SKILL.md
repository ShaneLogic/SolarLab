# Physics Literature Domain Classification

Use this skill when conducting literature reviews, organizing references by research area, or identifying seminal works in specific physics subdomains.

## When to Use
- Building domain-specific reference collections
- Conducting comprehensive literature reviews
- Identifying key authors in research subfields
- Categorizing physics references for database organization

## Classification Workflow

### 1. Scan Reference Elements
Examine titles, authors, and publication venues for domain-specific keywords.

### 2. Apply Domain Heuristics
Classify references into subdomains using these keyword patterns:

#### Semiconductor Devices & Technology
**Keywords:** Devices, Solar Cells, Photovoltaics, Transistors, Diodes
**Key Authors:** S.M. Sze, K. Takahashi
**Example References:**
- S.M. Sze, *Physics of Semiconductor Devices*
- K. Takahashi, *Amorphous Silicon Solar Cells*

#### Surface Science & Interfaces
**Keywords:** Surface, Interface, Photoemission, EXAFS, Adsorption, Reconstruction
**Key Authors:** W.E. Spicer, J. Tersoff, E. Taglauer
**Example References:**
- W.E. Spicer (Photoemission/Surfaces)
- J. Tersoff (Surface theory)
- E. Taglauer, *Semiconductor Interfaces*

#### Fundamental Solid State Theory
**Keywords:** Theory, Defects, Excitons, Statistical Mechanics, Band Structure
**Key Authors:** A.M. Stoneham, A. Sommerfeld, R.A. Stradling
**Example References:**
- A.M. Stoneham, *Theory of Defects in Solids*
- R. Peierls, *Quantum Theory of Solids*
- J.C. Phillips, *Bonds and Bands in Semiconductors*

#### Amorphous Materials
**Keywords:** Amorphous, Non-Crystalline, Disordered, Glass
**Key Authors:** R.A. Street, W.E. Spear, S.R. Ovshinsky, J. Stuke
**Example References:**
- R.A. Street, *Hydrogenated Amorphous Silicon*
- W.E. Spear (Amorphous Si)
- S.R. Ovshinsky (Switching phenomena)

#### Characterization Techniques
**Keywords:** Microscopy, Spectroscopy, EXAFS, STM, TEM, Diffraction
**Key Authors:** J. Stöhr, J. Stroscio, J.B. Pendry
**Example References:**
- J. Stöhr (EXAFS)
- J. Stroscio, *Scanning Tunneling Microscopy*
- J.B. Pendry, *Low Energy Electron Diffraction*

#### Crystallography & Growth
**Keywords:** Crystal Growth, Epitaxy, Defects, Structure, Lattice
**Key Authors:** B. Pamplin, W.G. Pfann, D.W. Pashley
**Example References:**
- B. Pamplin, *Crystal Growth*
- W.G. Pfann, *Zone Melting*

### 3. Map Authors to Domains
For frequently appearing authors, establish primary field mappings based on publication frequency and titles.
